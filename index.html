Because art imitates life. 

Here in drama, we practice the ancient art of improvisation. Without knowing, we use improvisation (improv for short) in our daily lives! Presentation you forgot about? Improv! Conversation with your crush? Improv! Trying to hide a bad exam score? Dissapointment. But also improv!
